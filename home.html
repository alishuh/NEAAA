What can you do?

Spotistats is a web application that allows you to view your Spotify statistics and get recommendations based on your listening habits.

Look at your statistics by clicking the Statistics Viewer link in the navigation bar above.

Get recommendations based on your listening habits by clicking the Recommendations link in the navigation bar above.

Take a quiz to see how well you know your music by clicking the Quiz link in the navigation bar above.
